This is a list of [DLLs](DLL "wikilink") avaliable for
[fenix](fenix "wikilink"). For more information, see
[DLL](DLL "wikilink"). For a list of documented DLL's, see
[DLLs](DLLs "wikilink").

-   [Explosion DLL](Explosion_DLL "wikilink") Explosion generator.
-   [GUI DLL](GUI_DLL "wikilink") Adds functionality for various GUI
    elements such as windows and textboxes.
-   [Image DLL](Image_DLL "wikilink") Allows other image formats to be
    used in [fenix](fenix "wikilink") such as jpeg.
-   [Mixer DLL](Mixer_DLL "wikilink") Various sound functions
-   [MPEG DLL](MPEG_DLL "wikilink") (requires "smpeg.dll") Allows MPEG
    video playback.
-   [Network DLL](Network_DLL "wikilink") (requires "SDL\_net.dll" and
    "Network.fh" files) Adds network functionality using TCP.
-   [LCD DLL](LCD_DLL "wikilink") Adds functionality for the Logitech
    G15 LCD screen.
-   [TTF DLL](TTF_DLL "wikilink") (requires "freetype.dll") Lets
    TrueType Fonts be used.
