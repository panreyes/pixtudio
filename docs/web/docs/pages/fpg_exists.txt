Definition
----------

**INT** fpg\_exists ( &lt;**INT** FileID&gt; )

Checks if an FPG exists with the specified [FileID](FileID "wikilink").

Parameters
----------

  ---------------- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **INT** FileID   - The [FileID](FileID "wikilink") of the [File](File "wikilink") to check for existence.
  ---------------- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Returns
-------

**INT** : Whether the [File](File "wikilink") exists

  --------------------------- ---------------------------------------------------------
  [false](false "wikilink")   - The specified [File](File "wikilink") does not exist.
  [true](true "wikilink")     - The specified [File](File "wikilink") exists.
  --------------------------- ---------------------------------------------------------
