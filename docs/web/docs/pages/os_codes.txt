Definition
----------

OS codes are codes used for the [global
variable](global_variable "wikilink") [os\_id](os_id "wikilink"), to
specify the current platform [BGDI](BGDI "wikilink") is running on.

OS codes 1000 and above are used for unofficial ports and may not be
available in the official Bennu version.
