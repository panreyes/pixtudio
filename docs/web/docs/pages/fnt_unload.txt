Definition
----------

**INT** fnt\_unload ( &lt;**INT** fontID&gt; )

Unloads the specified [font](font "wikilink") from memory.

The previous name [unload\_fnt](unload_fnt "wikilink")() is deprecated.

Parameters
----------

  ---------------- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **INT** fontID   - The [fontID](fontID "wikilink") of the [file](file "wikilink") to unload.
  ---------------- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Returns
-------

**INT** : [false](false "wikilink")
