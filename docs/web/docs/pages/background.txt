Description
-----------

The background is a [graphic](graphic "wikilink"), in the [system
file](system_file "wikilink") ([fileID](fileID "wikilink") of *0*) with
a [graphID](graphID "wikilink") equal to
[BACKGROUND](BACKGROUND "wikilink").

Examples in use:

-   `[[map_clear]](0,[[BACKGROUND]]);`, to clear the background to black
    (same as [screen\_clear](screen_clear "wikilink")).
-   `[[drawing_map]](0,[[BACKGROUND]]);`, to set drawing to
    the background.
