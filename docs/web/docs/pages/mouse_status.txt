Definition
----------

**INT** mouse\_status

**mouse\_status** is a [predefined](predefined "wikilink") [global
variable](global_variable "wikilink"), holding whether or not the mouse
cursor is inside the Bennu window.

  --------------------------- -------------------------------------------------
  *Value*                     - *Description*
  [false](false "wikilink")   - The mouse cursor is outside the Bennu window.
  [true](true "wikilink")     - The mouse cursor is inside the Bennu window.
  --------------------------- -------------------------------------------------
