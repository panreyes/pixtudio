Blitting is the operation of drawing one graph onto another, using
so-called bit blitting operations ([see Wikipedia on bit
blitting](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bit_blit)).

[Map functions](:Category:Maps "wikilink") like [put](put "wikilink")()
and [map\_xputnp](map_xputnp "wikilink")() are examples of
[functions](functions "wikilink") using blit operations.
