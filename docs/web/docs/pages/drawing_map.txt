Definition
----------

**INT** drawing\_map ( &lt;**INT** fileID&gt; , &lt;**INT** graphID&gt;
)

Tells [Bennu](Bennu "wikilink") to draw the coming
[drawings](drawing "wikilink") on a certain
[graphic](graphic "wikilink").

In order to draw with a certain z value again,
[drawing\_z](drawing_z "wikilink")() can be used.

Parameters
----------

  ----------------- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **INT** fileID    - The [fileID](fileID "wikilink") of the [file](file "wikilink") that holds the graphic.
  **INT** graphID   - The [graphID](graphID "wikilink") of the [graphic](graphic "wikilink") to draw on.
  ----------------- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Returns
-------

**INT** : [true](true "wikilink")
